This app is a poll management system with convenient and intuitive interface. It has complete and convenient CRUD functionality for polls, questions and answers. There's also ability to upload and parse an Excel file with which to import questions and their possible answers.

  • CRUD functionality
  • Excel file parsing
  • Front facing pages to show the polls to users and collect answers
  • Roles and authentication
  • Statistics
