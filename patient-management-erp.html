Management system for a surgical clinic

Javascript, HTML, CSS, PHP, React.js, Laravel, CI/CD, GitLab

  1. DigidWorks
  2. Projects
  3. Management system for a surgical clinic

This is a web-based management system customised especially for the needs of a surgical clinic. The system has different modules, working together.

Some of the features include:

  • CRUD for every object that the clinic deals with, e.g.:
    • Employees
    • Patients
    • Tasks
    • Surgical teams
    • Surgeries
    • Surgeons
    • Patient transportation
    • Logistics
    • Cleaning
    • Others
  • Management functions, e.g.:
    • Request clean up in a specified room
    • Give tasks to employees
    • Check on drivers and give directions
    • Complete patient management
  • Monitoring functions, e.g.:
    • Driver status, position, timing, etc.
    • Patient status - how they feel, do they need something, are they forbidden to have something, etc.
    • Cleaning - tracking the status of different rooms
    • Surgical teams - tasks, status, etc.
  • Real time messaging and help requests from patients
  • Audio notifications
  • Patient charts and statistics
  • Roles and Auth
  • User profiles
