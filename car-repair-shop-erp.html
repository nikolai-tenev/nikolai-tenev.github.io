ERP system for large car repair shop

Javascript, HTML, CSS, PHP, React.js, Laravel, CI/CD, GitLab, Node.js

  1. DigidWorks
  2. Projects
  3. ERP system for large car repair shop

This is a web application that is a specialized ERP system. It meets the needs of a large car repair shop for resource management and monitoring. The main goals of the app are to help with tracking employee behaviour, to remind about expiring annual technical assessment, get data about the cars from different online third party APIs, etc. There's also a CRM module for client data and relations.

More interesting features include:

  • Connection to online APIs to check vehicle status - car inspection status, insurance
  • System to track the actual time, the workers spent working
  • Entering information in a convenient and quick way
  • Generating customized reports
  • Alarms that remind you to do something within a given deadline
